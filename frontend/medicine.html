Common Medications

Aspirin

Use: Pain relief, fever reduction, prevention of heart attacks

Dosage: 325-650mg every 4-6 hours

Side Effects: Stomach upset, bleeding risk

Paracetamol

Use: Pain relief and fever reduction

Dosage: 500-1000mg every 4-6 hours

Side Effects: Rare when taken as directed

Medicine Safety Guidelines

Storage

  • Keep medicines in a cool, dry place
  • Store away from direct sunlight
  • Keep out of reach of children
  • Check expiration dates regularly

Precautions

  • Follow prescribed dosage strictly
  • Don't share medications
  • Inform doctor about allergies
  • Report side effects promptly

Timing

  • Take medicines at prescribed times
  • Complete full course of antibiotics
  • Don't skip doses
  • Set reminders if needed
